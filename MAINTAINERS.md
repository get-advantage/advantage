## Becoming a Maintainer

We welcome community members who want to take on more responsibility! To become a maintainer:

1. Make consistent, quality contributions over at least 3 months
2. Demonstrate good judgment and community engagement
3. Be nominated by an existing maintainer or self-nominate
4. Review by the maintainer committee

See [GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md#becoming-a-maintainer) for detailed criteria and the nomination process.

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Maintainers are expected to:
- ✅ Review pull requests within 1 week
- ✅ Respond to issues and community questions
- ✅ Participate in maintainer discussions
- ✅ Follow the [Code of Conduct](CODE-OF-CONDUCT.md)
- ✅ Maintain documentation in their areas
- ✅ Help mentor new contributors
